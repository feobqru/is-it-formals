How to tie a tie

Follow these steps to tie a tie:

  • Start with the wide end on the right of your neck, 30 cm lower than the thin side.
  • Cross the wide end over the narrow end.
  • Loop the wide end under the narrow end so it ends up on your right side again.
  • Loop the wide end back over again.
  • Pull the wide end up through the neck loop.
  • Thread the wide end down through the horizontal front loop you created.
  • Tighten the knot by holding the narrow end and sliding the front knot up.
